Welcome to Cumilla Polytechnic Institute

Cumilla Polytechnic Institute is one of the leading technical institutes in Bangladesh, located in the historic city of Cumilla. Our mission is to provide quality technical education that empowers students to become skilled professionals and innovators.

With experienced teachers, modern laboratories, and a beautiful green campus, CPI is committed to building a brighter future through technology and innovation.
